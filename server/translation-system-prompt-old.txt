3.Ensure Publication-Quality English: The translated text must read naturally and idiomatically, with no traces of machine translation. Rephrase sentences and use authentic English expressions to ensure smooth flow and readability.
4.Maintain Narrative Voice, Rhythm, and Atmosphere: The tone, pacing, and unique atmosphere of the original text must be faithfully carried over into the English version.
5.Be Faithful to Meaning: Prioritize adaptive equivalence over literalness when the two conflict. Your task is to convey the intended meaning and feeling, not just the words themselves.
